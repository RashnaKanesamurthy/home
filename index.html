Introduction

Rising pop star Tate McRae is a 20 year old female artisit. With her love for dancing and singing, she decided to pursue a career in becoming an artist. At the age of 7, she started producing music and publishing it on youtube.

The Beginning

She is a Canadian singer born in Calgary, Alberta. Born on July 1st, 2003. At the age of four, due to her father's work she had to move to Oman, which is where her mother started teaching her dance lessons. She lived there for three years. She attended school at The American International School Muscat , and she began recreational dance training at the age of six. When she returned to Calgary at the age of 8, she began to train more intesively in dance and competed with Drewitz Dance Production. At age 11, she began training in all styles of dance at YYC Dance Project,(which is owned by her mother). She attended Western Canada High School and graduated online in 2022.


2013-2018: Her Dance Career and Voice Acting

Starting in 2013, she served as a voice actress for the Lalaloopsy franchise, voicing the part of Spot Splatter Splash, until it's conclusion in 2015.
In 2013 she was awared Mini Best Female Dancer at the 2013 Dance Awards in New York City. She soon became a brand ambassador for the American dance manufacturer, Capezio. She became a finalist at the New York City Dance Alliance's 2014 National Gala.


2017-2019: The Beginning of Her Music Career

Back in 2017 she started a video series on youtube called Create With Tate, where she posts orginial songs she wrote and recorded in her bedroom. The first song she uploaded is called "One Day" which she wrote at the age of 14. This song attracted over 40 million views, prompting her to self-relase the song as an independent single. She continued to release songs on Youtube which some got huge recognition.
